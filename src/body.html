Our Director

Neetesh Kumar is a trailblazer in the field of political strategy, with over a decade of turning bold ideas into winning campaigns. Their journey began with a simple conviction - that disciplined data and sharp execution could reshape how elections are fought and won.

An early innovator in the space, they have built and led organizations at the forefront of political technology, pioneering tools and methods that bring rigor, transparency, and measurable accountability to public life.

Having advised leaders and parties across the country, their expertise spans the full arc of a modern campaign - from research and narrative to ground operations and the war room.

In 2022, they founded Inciterz, which has rapidly grown into one of the most trusted names in political consulting - built on a single promise: results, not rhetoric.
